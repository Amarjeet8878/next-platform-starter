Full Truck Load (FTL)

Reliable. Efficient. Nationwide.

We offer dedicated Full Truck Load (FTL) services for businesses that require exclusive use of a vehicle for large shipments. Whether transporting industrial goods or high-value cargo, we ensure:

  • Dedicated vehicle capacity
  • Direct route delivery (no transshipment)
  • Reduced transit time
  • Maximum safety for fragile cargo

With our fleet of 10+ owned trucks and 50+ partner vehicles, we guarantee smooth, scalable delivery across India.

Part Load Shipment (PTL)

Cost-Effective. Flexible. Smart Shipping.

Our PTL (Part Truck Load) solution is ideal for clients who need to move smaller loads efficiently without paying for an entire truck. We offer:

  • Shared truck space at reduced cost
  • Consolidated shipment handling
  • Scheduled dispatches from Pune and major hubs
  • Real-time tracking and support

Safe, punctual delivery — even when your shipment is small.

3PL (Third-Party Logistics) Services

Integrated Logistics. Simplified Operations.

Jay Balaji Cargo offers 3PL services for companies looking to outsource their entire logistics process. We handle everything from warehousing to delivery:

  • Inventory management & dispatch
  • Warehousing at Moshi, Pune (Warehouse 1 & 2)
  • Daily order fulfillment
  • Transport coordination and last-mile delivery
  • Custom SLAs and contract-based service

Let us take charge of your supply chain while you focus on growth.

Customized Supply Chain Solutions

Tailored. Scalable. Built Around You.

Every customer is different. We design supply chain strategies around your specific product, volume, and destination needs:

  • Route planning & fleet allocation
  • Dedicated account handling
  • End-to-end logistics tracking
  • Flexible pickup, delivery, warehousing

We deliver transport solutions that support your business — not just move goods.
