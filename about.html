About Jay Balaji Cargo

Jay Balaji Cargo was established in 2019 by Mr. Bipin Mishra with a vision to simplify and strengthen logistics and transportation across India. Headquartered at:

Gate No. 269, 403 - Borhade Wadi, Flower City,
Moshi, Tal Khed, District - Pune, Maharashtra - 410501

Over the years, we have grown into a reliable and well-connected logistics partner offering comprehensive solutions including:

  • Full Load Transportation
  • Part Load Shipment
  • 3PL (Third Party Logistics) Services
  • End-to-End Supply Chain Solutions

With a dedicated fleet of 10+ owned vehicles and a robust network of 50+ logistics partners, we provide seamless transport services to every corner of India. Our commitment to timely delivery, safety, and customer satisfaction has helped us build lasting relationships with clients from diverse industries.
